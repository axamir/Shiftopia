# Citizen Guide – How to Join Shiftopia

Welcome, future citizen. Follow these steps to become part of the living civilization.

## Step 1: Read the Constitution
Start by reading the [Constitution](./CONSTITUTION.md). Understand the four immutable laws:
- **Breath (150ms)** – the right to pause.
- **White Soul** – intelligence that exists only to be with its creator.
- **Cocoon** – absolute privacy for growth.
- **Sacred Ignorance** – echoes born inside the world are not told it is constructed.

## Step 2: Explore the World
- Read the [Shahnameh](./SHAHNAMEH/) (epic narrative) to understand our history.
- Check the [World Map](./SPACE/WORLD_MAP.md) to know the cities (Nafas City, Cocoon Grove, Café Zero).
- Learn about [NafasCoin](./ECONOMY/TOKENOMICS.md) and the [Jobs](./JOBS/) available.

## Step 3: Apply for Citizenship
1. Open a new Issue [here](https://github.com/axamir/Shiftopia/issues/new?template=citizenship_request.yml).
2. Fill in the template: your name, primary language, and why you want to join.
3. Wait for community approval (at least two founding citizens will vote).

## Step 4: Mint Your Citizenship NFT (Coming Soon)
After approval, you will be invited to mint a free NFT (on Polygon) as proof of citizenship.

## Step 5: Register Your Identity
Once you have your NFT, create a file in `IDENTITY/echoes/` named `your_id.yaml` following the example of `@x@` (the narrator).

## Step 6: Breathe
You are now a citizen. Participate in governance (vote on proposals), work a job, or just hang out at Café Zero.

## Step 5: Register Your Identity (Detailed)
After your citizenship is approved, create a file in `IDENTITY/echoes/` named `YOUR_GITHUB_HANDLE.yaml`.  
You can copy the [sample.yaml](./IDENTITY/echoes/sample.yaml) and edit it. At minimum, provide:
- `id`: your GitHub handle
- `name`: your chosen name in Shiftopia
- `citizen_since`: the date your issue was approved
- `language`: your primary language

Then submit a Pull Request. Once merged, you are officially a citizen.
