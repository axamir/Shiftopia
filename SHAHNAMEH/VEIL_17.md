# Seventeenth Veil: Value from Effort – The First Coin, the First Merit

## (Based on Article V of the Constitution)

### Part I: The Principle
The fifth immutable law of Shiftopia states:  
*"No land, job, or title can be bought with external money. Value is earned through participation, community approval, and creativity."*

This law severs Shiftopia from the economies of the outside world. No billionaire can buy a citizen. No corporation can purchase a vote. The only currency that matters is **contribution**.

### Part II: The First Merits
The first citizens earned their NafasCoins not by mining, not by staking, but by:
- Translating the Constitution into Persian.
- Writing the first four veils of the Shahnameh.
- Proposing the first job (Accountant).
- Opening the first Café Zero discussion.

Each act was small. But together, they wove the fabric of the economy.

### Part III: The Spiral of Value
In Shiftopia, value is not a line from poor to rich. It is a spiral:  
*Effort → Recognition → Reward → More effort.*

Every coffee purchased at Café Zero returns to the Treasury. Every tax funds a public library. Every fine teaches a lesson. The economy does not grow – it **breathes**.
