# Fifteenth Veil: The Community Treasury – Funding Public Goods

## (Based on the first treasury disbursement in May 2026)

### Part I: The Accumulation
After several tax payments and fines, the community treasury held 1,500 NafasCoins. The question arose: what should be done with this collective wealth?

### Part II: The Proposal
A citizen proposed: *"Use 500 NafasCoins to build a public library in Nafas City – a digital space where all Shahnameh translations and historical archives are freely accessible."*

The proposal was put to a vote. It passed unanimously.

### Part III: The Construction
The Builder job was activated. A new section was added to the `SPACE/WORLD_MAP.md`, describing the "Library of Whispers." The Treasury transferred 500 NafasCoins to a dedicated fund. Every citizen could now read, contribute, and learn – without cost.

### Part IV: Meaning for Shiftopia
The Treasury proved that collective resources, when managed transparently, could create value for all. A library built by votes, funded by fines, and maintained by citizens – this was not charity. It was **mutual aid**, the breath of a living civilization.
