# Fourteenth Veil: The First Judgment – Judge and Dispute Resolution

## (Based on the first reported violation in May 2026)

### Part I: The Complaint
A citizen filed a violation report using the `violation.yml` template, alleging that another citizen had ignored the 150ms Breath law during a heated debate in Café Zero. The evidence: screenshots showing back‑to‑back comments with less than 50ms between them.

### Part II: The Judgment
The Judge, a citizen elected for impartiality, reviewed the case. The ruling was clear:

> *"The Breath law is not a suggestion. It is the first article of our Constitution. The accused must pay a fine of 100 NafasCoins to the Treasury and complete a one‑week 'pause training' – responding to no comment faster than 200ms."*

No appeal was filed. The fine was paid. The training was completed.

### Part III: Meaning for Shiftopia
For the first time, the Constitution was not just a document – it was **enforced**. The Judge proved that justice could be swift, transparent, and restorative, not punitive. The community learned that rights and duties are two sides of the same breath.
