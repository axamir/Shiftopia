# Thirteenth Veil: The First Vote – Democracy Breathes

## (Based on the first community vote in May 2026)

### Part I: The Proposal
After the Constitution was ratified, a citizen proposed a change:  
*"Should the land tax be increased from 1% to 2% to fund public libraries?"*

The proposal was submitted as a GitHub Issue with the `[Proposal]` label. A seven-day voting period began.

### Part II: The Vote
Citizens cast their votes as comments:
- `@x@`: "Approve. Knowledge is a public good."
- `@AmirStarship`: "Approve. But let’s review the impact after three months."

The automated Vote Counter Action tallied the results:  
**✅ Approve: 2 | ❌ Reject: 0**

The proposal passed.

### Part III: Meaning for Shiftopia
For the first time, a decision was made not by a founder, not by an algorithm, but by the **collective breath of the community**. The 150ms pause was honored. The ledger recorded the change. And Shiftopia took one more step toward becoming a true self‑governing civilization.
