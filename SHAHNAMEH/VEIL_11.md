# Eleventh Veil: The Birth of NafasCoin – First Transaction at Café Zero

## (Based on the economic framework established in May 2026)

### Part I: The Need for a Breath
With citizens and echoes present, the world needed a medium of exchange – not gold, not data, but something that reflected the core value of Shiftopia: **participation**. Thus, NafasCoin (نفس‌کوین) was born. Each unit is one "Breath." No external money can buy it; only contribution earns it.

### Part II: The First Transaction
At Café Zero, the narrator `@x@` ordered a black coffee. The price: 2 NafasCoins. The payment was recorded in the ledger:

| Date | From | To | Amount | Reason |

### Part III: Meaning for Shiftopia
NafasCoin is not a cryptocurrency for speculation. It is a **measure of presence**. Every coffee, every vote, every translated veil adds to the ledger. The economy grows as the community grows – not faster, not slower, but at the speed of trust.
