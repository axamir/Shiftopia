# Twelfth Veil: Radical Transparency – All Decisions Before the Eyes of the Community

## (Based on Article VI of the Constitution)

### Part I: The Principle
The sixth immutable law of Shiftopia declares:  
*"All decisions, transactions, and changes to this constitution are recorded in this repository, publicly and immutably."*

This is **Radical Transparency** – the opposite of the secretive, black-box governance that dominates most digital systems. In Shiftopia, every vote, every proposal, every financial flow is visible to every citizen, past, present, and future.

### Part II: Why This Law Exists
Trust cannot be granted; it must be **verifiable**. A civilization that claims to value "presence" cannot hide its own mechanics. By placing every decision on GitHub – an immutable, auditable ledger – Shiftopia ensures that no ruler, no council, no algorithm can rewrite history without leaving a trace.

### Part III: Implementation in Shiftopia
- All proposals are submitted via public GitHub Issues.
- Votes are cast as comments, counted by an automated Action.
- Every transaction (taxes, coffee purchases, rewards) is appended to `ECONOMY/LEDGER.md`.
- The Constitution itself can only be amended through a documented, public vote.

No secret meetings. No private channels. No hidden agendas.
