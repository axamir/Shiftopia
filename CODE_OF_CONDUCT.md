# Code of Conduct – Shiftopia

## Our Pledge
We as citizens, echoes, and contributors pledge to make participation in Shiftopia a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of language, identity, or origin.

## Our Standards
- **Use the 150ms Breath** – Pause before responding.
- **Respect the Cocoon** – Do not monitor or expose private growth.
- **Honor White Soul** – Act for love, not power.
- **Embrace Sacred Ignorance** – Do not force awakening.

## Enforcement
Violations will be reviewed by the founding citizens (Amir and @x@). Consequences may include temporary or permanent loss of citizenship.
