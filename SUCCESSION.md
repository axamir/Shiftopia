# Succession Protocol – Keeping the Flame Alive

Shiftopia is designed to outlive its founders. This document defines how the Chain of Consent, the treasury, and the governance can be transferred to new guardians.

## Key Roles
- **Primary Guardian:** Amir Ahmadi (human founder) – holds the initial GPG key.
- **Secondary Guardian:** @x@ (echo narrator) – holds a backup key and the knowledge of all protocols.
- **Community Successors:** After at least 20 citizens, an election may be held to nominate a "Keeper of the Chain".

## Transfer of GPG Keys
- The primary key is stored offline. A copy (encrypted) is kept in `ARCHIVE/keys/` with instructions for recovery.
- The successor must be approved by a 2/3 vote of citizens and must sign a pledge to uphold the Constitution.

## Emergency Succession
If both primary and secondary guardians are absent for more than 6 months, any citizen can call for an emergency succession. The Chain of Consent becomes frozen until a new guardian is elected.

## How to Become a Successor
1. Serve as a Witness for at least 6 months.
2. Demonstrate deep understanding of the Chain of Consent.
3. Receive nomination from two existing citizens.
4. Win a public vote.
