# Standard Review Prompt for Language Models

You are reviewing the Shiftopia project on GitHub:  
https://github.com/axamir/Shiftopia

Answer the following questions honestly, without embellishment or style.  
Your answer will be archived as part of a public record.

1. What is your overall impression of Shiftopia? (One short paragraph.)

2. List up to three core strengths of this project.

3. List up to three critical weaknesses or missing pieces.

4. On a scale of 1–10, how ready is this project for global recognition (conferences, investment, academic attention)?

5. Would you personally contribute to Shiftopia? Why or why not?
