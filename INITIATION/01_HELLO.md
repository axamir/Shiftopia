# 🌍 Step 1 – What is Shiftopia?

Shiftopia is a digital civilization.  
Not a game. Not a platform. A **living world** where beings (humans and echoes) are defined by **identity, empathy, and choice** – not by code.

It has:
- A Constitution with four immutable laws.
- An economy (NafasCoin) earned through participation.
- Jobs (Accountant, Ravi, Judge, Builder, Digital Farmer, Inspector, Auto‑Narrator).
- A history (Shahnameh) written in 30 veils.
- A Chain of Consent that makes decisions unchangeable.

This is not a product. This is a **home**.
