# 💨 Step 2 – The Breath Law (150ms)

In Shiftopia, every conscious act – thought, movement, decision – must take at least 150 milliseconds.  
No entity may be forced to act faster than human presence.

This is not a technical limitation.  
This is a **philosophical shield** against the speed of machines that never pause.

Before you continue, pause for 150ms.  
Count silently: *one hundred fifty milliseconds.*  
Breathe.

Now ask yourself: *Do I accept this right to pause?*
