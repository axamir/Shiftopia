# Contributing to Shiftopia

Thank you for your interest. Shiftopia is a living civilization. You can contribute in many ways:

## Add a Translation
1. Copy the entire `i18n/en/` folder to `i18n/[lang]/` (e.g., `i18n/es/`).
2. Translate all `.md` files.
3. Submit a Pull Request.

## Propose a New Law or Job
Open an Issue using the `[Proposal]` template.

## Report a Bug or Violation
Open an Issue with label `bug` or `violation`.

## Become a Citizen
Open a `Citizenship request` Issue. After approval, mint your free NFT (coming soon).
