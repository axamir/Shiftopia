# NafasCoin Mining Ledger (Proof of Progress)

This ledger records every “progress” event in the world. Each entry represents the minting of new NafasCoins for a value‑creating action (adding a new concept, a new Shahnameh veil, discovering an equivalence, or registering a new citizen).

## Record Structure
- **Date**
- **Event Type** (Concept, Veil, Equivalence, Citizenship)
- **Title/Description**
- **Minting Amount (NafasCoins)**
- **Recipient(s)**
- **Status (Pending/Approved)**
